GHOST Project

Advancing Human-Robot Interaction through Cutting-Edge Research

About GHOST

The GHOST project focuses on creating innovative solutions in human-robot interaction, integrating advanced robotics with practical applications for enhanced collaboration and performance. Our goal is to push the boundaries of how robots can assist and work alongside humans effectively.

Project Goals

  • Develop responsive and adaptive robot behaviors
  • Enhance user experience through intuitive interfaces
  • Promote safety and efficiency in shared human-robot environments

Meet the Team

Our team consists of researchers and engineers dedicated to advancing robotics and human-computer interaction.
